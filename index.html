About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Ethics, Governance and Society (EGS) at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, under the supervision of Alessandra Palmigiano and Alexander Kurz.

My main research interests lie in Theoretical Computer Science, especially in:

  • Category Theory,
  • Universal Algebra and Coalgebra,
  • Logic and Artificial Intelligence.
